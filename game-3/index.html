Tutorial

How to Play

1
Tap your blue star to select it
A glowing ring appears. You can then tap any other star to send half your troops there.
2
Capture stars to grow your fleet
Each star you own automatically generates troops. Capture more to grow faster. Grey stars are neutral — easier to take.
3
Most stars when time runs out → WINS
The 60-second countdown shows at the top. Hold more than half the stars when it hits 0 to win.
4
Drag the map to explore
The map is larger than the screen. Drag on empty space to pan the view. The mini-map (bottom-right) shows your position.
Special Star Types
⚡ Pulsar
Fires a shockwave every 8s that destroys nearby enemy troops.
🌫 Nebula
Troop count hidden as "?" — buy Quantum Lens to reveal.
◎ White Dwarf
Small but tough. Blocks 50% of incoming damage.
🔴 Red Giant
Generates troops 3× faster but has a low capacity cap.
💥
Supernova: Double-tap your star at 80%+ capacity
Blasts 30% of troops to ALL nearby stars at once — great for a surprise multi-capture.
